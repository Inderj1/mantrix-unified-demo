Component Consolidation & Optimization

THE KEY DIFFERENTIATOR: Aggregate component needs across all FGs

üéØ Why Consolidation Matters

Traditional MRP Problem: Creates separate purchase requisitions for each finished good, leading to small-quantity orders, no volume discounts, high admin overhead, and fragmented supplier relationships.

STOX.AI Solution: Consolidates component requirements across ALL finished goods, creating ONE optimized purchase order per component with volume discounts, reduced shipping costs, simplified inventory management, and stronger supplier partnerships.
