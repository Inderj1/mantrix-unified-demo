AI-Powered Status Changes
How AI Detects Updates ‚Äî Without the Clerk Checking
Mantrix continuously monitors SAP for changes that affect parked or routed invoices. When something changes, it appears as a notification in Tile 5. The clerk doesn't have to check. The work comes to them.

GR Posted Detection
Invoice was parked because EKPO-WEBRE = 'X' and no GR existed. Mantrix monitors MATDOC for movement type 101 against this PO line. When GR appears ‚Üí notification: "GR posted. Ready to re-process." Clerk clicks ‚Üí opens Tile 1 with GR data pre-loaded. One-click post.
