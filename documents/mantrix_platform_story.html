Philosophy
Platform Design Principles
These aren't aspirational. They're hard rules baked into every agent, every screen, every decision.
01
One Screen = One Decision
No clicking through 6 Fiori apps. Every screen presents a decision with context, confidence, and a recommended action.
02
AI Explains Before You Ask
Every autonomous action comes with a natural-language explanation. Hover = explanation, not tooltip. Auditors replay decisions like chess moves.
03
Read from SAP Truth
Agents read from standard SAP tables (MATDOC, EKET, EKBE, ACDOCA). No shadow databases. No data lakes. SAP is the single source of truth.
04
Act Through Standard BAPIs
Every write operation uses SAP standard BAPIs. No custom ABAP. No direct table updates. Upgrade-safe. Audit-safe.
05
Configurable, Not Coded
IT configures thresholds, rules, and agent behavior through the platform â€” not code. No consultants needed for changes.
06
Closed-Loop Learning
Every human override, every approval, every rejection trains the model. Agents get smarter. Thresholds self-adjust. The platform learns your business.
