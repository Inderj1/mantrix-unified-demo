How AI Prioritizes the Queue
AI Prioritization Logic
The queue is not sorted by date. It's sorted by a weighted priority score that considers financial impact, payment urgency, processing complexity, and aging. The clerk doesn't need to guess what's important ‚Äî the highest-impact items surface to the top. Exceptions that block payment runs are always prioritized above routine postings.
üí∞ Financial Impact
Higher dollar amounts rank higher. A $312K invoice blocking a payment run outranks a $1.8K routine posting.
Weight: 35%
‚è∞ Payment Urgency
Invoices approaching payment deadlines, especially those with early-pay discounts (2/10 Net 30), rank higher.
Weight: 25%
üìã Processing Complexity
Exceptions and partial matches surface above clean matches. The clerk handles complex items when fresh, routine items later.
Weight: 20%
üìÖ Aging & SLA
Invoices stuck longer than SLA targets get boosted. Parked items that have been waiting for responses get escalated.
Weight: 20%
